Importance of Healthy Lifestyle

1. Healthy Eating

Eating nutritious food helps in maintaining body strength and energy. A balanced diet includes fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and enough water.

2. Regular Exercise

Physical activity keeps your body fit and improves mental health. Daily exercise helps reduce stress and boosts confidence.

Quick Tip

Try to walk at least 30 minutes daily and drink plenty of water to stay active and refreshed.
